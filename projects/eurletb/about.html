Our Story

15 Years of Building
Algeria's Infrastructure

Founded in 2009 by Toufik Achouri, our company began as a small contracting firm with a clear mission — to deliver construction projects that prioritize quality, safety, and client satisfaction above all else. Over the years, we have grown into a fully integrated construction group trusted by both private clients and public institutions across Algeria.

2009 — Founded

EURL ETB Achouri Toufik established in Blida, Algeria, with a focus on residential construction.

2013 — Expansion

Expanded into commercial and industrial projects, growing the team to over 30 engineers and technicians.

2018 — Certification

Achieved ISO 9001 quality management certification and launched our heavy machinery division.

2024 — Today

240+ projects completed, 50+ expert engineers, and a reputation for delivering on time, every time.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver construction projects of the highest quality — on time and within budget — while maintaining the safety of our workforce and the trust of our clients. We are committed to building structures that last generations and relationships that endure.

Our Vision

To become Algeria's most trusted construction group — recognized not only for the scale of our projects but for the integrity with which we execute them. We envision a future where every community we build in is stronger because of our presence.
